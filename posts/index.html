Event Overview

Legwork for Lungs is an annual 5K run and 1-mile walk to raise funds for lung cancer research at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. This year’s event promises a day filled with energy, hope, and community spirit.

Date & Location

🗓️ Sunday, September 29, 2024
📍 Buccleuch Park, New Brunswick, NJ

Schedule

  • 8:00 AM – Check-In Opens
  • 9:00 AM – 5K Race Starts
  • 9:15 AM – 1-Mile Walk Starts
  • 10:00 AM – Refreshments, Music, and Awards

Registration

Registration is $25 and includes a T-shirt and goodie bag. All proceeds benefit lung cancer research at MSK.
